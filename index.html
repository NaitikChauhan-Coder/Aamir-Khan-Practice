Brief Intoduction of Aamir Khan

Aamir Khan

Aamir Khan is an acclaimed Indian actor, filmmaker, and television personality who has been a dominant force in Hindi cinema for over three decades. Widely celebrated by the media as "Mr. Perfectionist," he is renowned for his meticulous method acting, dedication to strong scripts, and focus on socially relevant narratives.

His ability to choose impactful projects has resulted in some of the highest-grossing films in Indian history, including blockbusters like 3 Idiots, PK, and the sports biopic Dangal.

Beyond starring in iconic films, Aamir Khan has directed and produced acclaimed works through his company, Aamir Khan Productions. His storytelling often blends entertainment with social commentary, which is prominently highlighted by his philanthropic efforts with the Paani Foundation and his impactful television talk show, Satyamev Jayate. For his profound contributions to the arts, he has been honored with India’s prestigious civilian awards, the Padma Shri (2003) and the Padma Bhushan (2010).


His Top Films

Dangal

Directed by Nitesh Tiwari, Dangal stars Aamir Khan and stands as one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.

The Plot: It tells the inspiring true story of Mahavir Singh Phogat, a former amateur wrestler who defies societal norms by training his daughters, Geeta Phogat and Babita Kumari, to become India's first world-class female Commonwealth wrestling champions.

PK

Much like Dangal, PK is another record-breaking, critically acclaimed Bollywood blockbuster starring Aamir Khan.Released in 2014 and directed by Rajkumar Hirani, it was the first Indian film to cross the ₹7 billion milestone worldwide.

The Plot: It follows the story of a man who loses his memory and embarks on a journey to rediscover his identity while questioning the nature of belief and reality.
